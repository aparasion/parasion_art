Bolesław Parasion

sculptor artist

My name is Bolesław Parasion, and I am a sculptor. My work focuses on sacred and folk themes. I create Triptychs, colorful angels, Madonnas, Christmas nativity scenes, and monumental religious sculptures, such as The Creation of the World, The Last Supper, and The Way of the Cross. Several of my Way of the Cross works are displayed in churches in Poland and the USA. My works have been exhibited both in Poland and abroad and have been recognized in art competitions. I continuously create, fulfilling commissions for collectors, private clients, and art galleries. Sculpture remains my greatest passion and an inseparable part of my life. I invite you to explore my gallery of works.
